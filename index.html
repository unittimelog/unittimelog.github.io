UnitTimeLog - iOS app
‹
UnitTimeLog is a simple timer app that keeps track of how many hours you worked on a project basis.
The timer stops at the end of repetitive intervals and reminds you to resume.
You can add projects as many as you need. 2 taps to switch the active project.
Your logged hours are displayed on bar charts in 4 different periods.
The Cycle chart lets you set one-time or repetitive closing dates for your billing cycles
UnitTimeLog uses your iCloud space to store data accessible from different devices.
UnitTimeLog supports Dark Mode for ambient lighting conditions or users' preferences.
